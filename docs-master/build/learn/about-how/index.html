How Fan Stories works

Fan Stories is simple to understand. Build your website. Publish blogs everyday through Fan stories and these are also shared on your social media. Enable monetization and make money.

Step 1 - Connect your Facebook account.

Connecting your Facebook account to Fan Stories enables us to post on your behalf, into your Facebook page. We don't post anything without your explicit permission.

Step 2 - Create a blog / page on Fan Stories

The next step is to create a blog or page on Fan Stories.

Step 3 - Compose a blog post

Create a blog post with your content on Fan Stories.

Step 4 - Post your content on to the blog. Share on Facebook.
