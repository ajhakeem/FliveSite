Better ads on a faster web.

We can’t make the web better if we don’t fix ads. Today’s ads fail to meet their goals because they are slow, unpredictable and disruptive. The open-source AMP Ads Initiative aims to make advertising on the web better with faster, lighter, and safer ad experiences, starting with ads and extending to landing pages.

Join Today

What are AMP Ads?

AMP Ads are a new, better way of building, delivering and measuring ads that are faster, lighter and more secure. AMP Ads are ads built using AMP-HTML and use many of the smart components that make AMP pages fast (like amp-analytics). These ads are only delivered after being validated, ensuring that they’re free of malware. Most of all, these ads can be delivered anywhere on the web, not just on AMP pages.

AMP Ads represent an opportunity to fix key issues with ad experiences on the web.
— Kavata Mbondo
Vice President of Digital Revenue Strategy and Operations, Time Inc.

Why AMP Ads?

Faster ads drive better performance

AMP Ads are 6x faster than regular ads on AMP pages, making them more viewable, more effective and more likely to perform well.

Lighter ads lead to better ad experiences

Heavy and unpredictable ad experiences slow down the page, cost users money, and can hurt your brand. AMP Ads are 3x lighter and the AMP framework guarantees that these ads only behave as intended so users always have a positive experience with your brand.

Safer ads build trust in your brands

AMP Ads need to be validated before they’re served, eliminating the risk of malware and building trust in the brands users are engaging with.
